##  Responsible Social Media Use Checklist

###  Do’s

* ☐ Think before posting
* ☐ Keep your profile professional
* ☐ Check facts before sharing
* ☐ Use privacy settings properly
* ☐ Engage respectfully

###  Don’ts

* ☐ Share personal/sensitive information
* ☐ Post offensive or harmful content
* ☐ Believe or spread fake news
* ☐ Click on suspicious links
* ☐ Spend excessive time on social media
