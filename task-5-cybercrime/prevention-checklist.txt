A. Quick Safety Checklist
☐ I use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
☐ I have enabled Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
☐ I avoid clicking on unknown or suspicious links
☐ I do not share personal or sensitive information publicly
☐ I avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or important logins
☐ My devices are regularly updated with latest security patches
☐ I never share UPI PIN, OTP, or banking credentials
☐ I verify all UPI payment requests before approving
☐ I do not scan unknown QR codes
☐ I always log out from shared/public devices
------------------------------------------------------------------
B. Key Prevention Tips (Top 6)
Use Strong Passwords & Enable 2FA:
Combine complex passwords with two-factor authentication for maximum account security.
Stay Alert to Phishing Attacks:
Never click suspicious links or download unknown attachments from emails/SMS.
Protect Personal Information:
Avoid oversharing sensitive details like phone numbers, addresses, or college IDs online.
Practice Safe Internet Usage:
Do not access important accounts on public Wi-Fi; always keep your devices updated.
Ensure UPI & Financial Safety:
Never share your UPI PIN or OTP, and always verify payment requests before approving.
